# Growth Sprint

Execute a focused growth sprint to rapidly increase subscribers.

## Process

1. **Analyze Current State**:
   - Check current subscriber count
   - Review recent growth rate
   - Identify best-performing content

2. **Generate Tactics**:
   - Invoke `growth-hacker` for fresh tactics
   - Invoke `reddit-scout` for engagement opportunities
   - Invoke `seo-strategist` for quick-win keywords

3. **Create Growth Content**:
   - Viral content pieces
   - Shareable infographics
   - Interactive tools

4. **Distribution Blitz**:
   - Invoke `social-manager` for multi-platform push
   - Create Reddit engagement plan
   - Identify cross-promotion opportunities

5. **Track & Optimize**:
   - Set up tracking for sprint
   - Daily check on metrics
   - Double down on what works

## Sprint Options

- **Traffic Sprint**: Focus on SEO quick wins
- **Viral Sprint**: Create maximally shareable content
- **Community Sprint**: Deep engagement in Reddit/communities
- **Partnership Sprint**: Cross-promotions with other newsletters

## Output

- Prioritized list of growth activities
- Content pieces to create
- Daily action checklist
- Success metrics
